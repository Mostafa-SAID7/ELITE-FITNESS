# HIT Egypt - Product Roadmap

Strategic roadmap for HIT Egypt fitness coaching platform development with planned features across all phases.

---

## 🎯 Product Vision

HIT Egypt aims to be Egypt's leading digital fitness platform, connecting professional trainers with members through innovative technology. Our roadmap focuses on building a scalable, feature-rich platform that supports:

- 📱 Seamless booking and member management
- 💳 Secure payment processing
- 📊 Analytics and business intelligence
- 🤖 AI-powered personalization
- 🌐 Multi-channel experiences (web, mobile, wearables)
